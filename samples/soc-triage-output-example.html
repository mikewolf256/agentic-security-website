What Every Alert Gets

  • Clear Verdict: CRITICAL / FALSE_POSITIVE / NEEDS_INVESTIGATION
  • Confidence Score: 0-100% based on pattern strength + context
  • Reasoning Chain: "Why we made this decision" in plain English
  • MITRE ATT&CK Tags: Tactics, techniques, sub-techniques with clickable URLs
  • Recommended Action: Auto-hold, escalate, or dismiss
  • Complete Audit Trail: Timestamp, context used, PII scrubbing logs

Why This Works

Ownership Context

We track which resources each user owns. Traditional systems flag "user accessed 10 resources" and trigger alerts. We ask: "did they access OTHER users' resources?" This eliminates 90% of false positives from legitimate multi-resource users.

Business Context

System learns your environment: QA users, recent deployments, known tools. A sequential access pattern 2 hours after a deployment with qa_automation tags = false positive, not an attack.

PII Protection

All emails, IPs, and sensitive data scrubbed before AI analysis. GDPR/PCI-DSS compliant. Customer privacy protected while preserving detection accuracy.

Audit-Ready

Every decision logged with complete context: what data was used, what was scrubbed, confidence score, reasoning chain. Perfect for SOC2/compliance audits.
