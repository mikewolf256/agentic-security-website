How it works

AI-powered discovery with human-validated findings. We identify vulnerabilities. We don't exploit your production systems.

1) Automated Discovery

AI-powered reconnaissance maps your attack surface: endpoints, APIs, JavaScript files, configurations. We discover what's exposed before testing.

2) Targeted Testing

Comprehensive automated scanning across all OWASP Top 10 categories. Safe, rate-limited testing that identifies vulnerabilities without exploiting production data.

3) Human Validation

Security expert reviews every critical finding. We verify exploitability, assess real-world impact, and write specific remediation steps.

4) Secure Delivery

Reports delivered with SHA256 integrity verification. Optional password protection. Attestation letter included in Standard+ (add-on available). Data deleted per your retention requirements.

What we do (and don't do)

✓ We identify & validate:

  • Proof of Concept demonstrating exploitability
  • Evidence: request/response, screenshots, reproduction steps
  • Business impact assessment for each finding
  • Specific remediation recommendations

We never:

  • Exfiltrate or access real customer data
  • Perform denial-of-service attacks
  • Modify production databases
  • Leave persistent backdoors or test artifacts
  • Attempt to bypass safeguards outside agreed scope

Comprehensive Testing Aligned to OWASP Top 10 (2025)

Every assessment includes comprehensive testing aligned to the latest OWASP Top 10 risk categories.

A01 Broken Access Control (IDOR, privilege escalation)
A02 Security Misconfiguration (headers, secrets, CORS)
A03 Software Supply Chain (CVEs, outdated components)
A04 Cryptographic Failures (weak crypto, exposed keys)
A05 Injection (SQL, XSS, Command, SSRF, XXE)
A06 Insecure Design (business logic, race conditions)
A07 Authentication Failures (JWT, OAuth, session)
A08 Data Integrity Failures (deserialization, uploads)
A09 Logging & Monitoring Failures
A10 Exception Handling (ReDoS, error disclosure)

Enterprise-Grade Data Security

Your credentials and findings are protected with the same rigor we'd expect for our own systems:

Credential Protection

  • AES-256 encrypted vault: Credentials encrypted at rest with per-client isolation
  • Secure transfer: 1Password, Vault, or encrypted channel
  • Time-limited access: Only during the assessment window
  • Multi-pass secure deletion: Cryptographically destroyed after engagement
  • Access logging: Every credential access logged with checksums

Data Lifecycle Management

  • Per-client isolation: Your data never mixes with other clients
  • Complete audit trail: Tamper-evident logging for compliance
  • Configurable retention: Data deleted per your requirements (default: 30 days)
  • Deletion certificates: Signed proof of secure data destruction
  • Emergency stop: Instant kill switch if you need to halt testing
